Integration Guide

📌

Pinterest Catalogs

  1. Open Pinterest Business Hub → Catalogs
  2. Click "Create Catalog" → "RSS / ATOM feed"
  3. Paste your feed URL (use US feed for USD pricing)
  4. Set update frequency to Daily
  5. Submit and wait for Pinterest to crawl your products
🛒

Google Merchant Center

  1. Open Merchant Center → Products → Feeds
  2. Click "+" → choose "Scheduled fetch"
  3. Enter a feed name and your target country/language
  4. Paste the corresponding regional feed URL
  5. Set fetch frequency to Every 24 hours

Feed Schema (Google Shopping RSS)

<rss version="2.0" xmlns:g="http://base.google.com/ns/1.0">
  <channel>
    <item>
      <g:id>          variant ID
      <title>         product + variant name
      <description>   plain-text description (max 5000 chars)
      <link>          product URL with ?variant=ID
      <g:image_link>  primary image
      <g:additional_image_link>  (up to 5)
      <g:availability> in stock
      <g:price>       29.99 USD
      <g:condition>   new
      <g:brand>       vendor name
      <g:product_type> Shopify product type
      <g:item_group_id> parent product ID
      <g:mpn>         SKU (if present)
    </item>
  </channel>
</rss>
